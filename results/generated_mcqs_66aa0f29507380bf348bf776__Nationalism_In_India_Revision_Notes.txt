## MCQ 1
Question: What event led Mahatma Gandhi to call off the Non-Cooperation Movement?
A) The Jallianwala Bagh Massacre
B) The Chauri Chaura Incident
C) The passing of the Rowlatt Act
D) The formation of the Swaraj Party
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: Which of the following was NOT a demand in Gandhi's letter to Viceroy Irwin in 1930?
A) Abolition of the salt tax
B) Release of political prisoners
C) Complete withdrawal of the British from India
D) Reduction of land revenue
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question:  What did freedom mean for plantation workers in Assam during the Non-Cooperation Movement?
A) Ownership of the tea gardens
B) Higher wages and better living conditions
C) The right to move freely and maintain ties with their villages
D) Representation in legislative councils
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 4
Question: Who painted the famous image of Bharat Mata, inspired by the Swadeshi movement?
A) Bankim Chandra Chattopadhyay
B) Rabindranath Tagore
C) Abanindranath Tagore
D) Natesa Sastri
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 5
Question: What was the main reason for the Muslim community's support for the British during World War I?
A) Promise of independence after the war
B) Assurance of protection for the sacred places of the Ottoman Empire.
C) Financial incentives offered by the British government
D) Shared animosity towards Germany and Austria
Correct Answer: B
