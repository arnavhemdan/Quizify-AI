## MCQ 1
Question: Which part of the nervous system controls voluntary actions like talking and writing?
A) Midbrain
B) Hindbrain
C) Forebrain
D) Spinal cord
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 2
Question: What is the function of abscisic acid in plants?
A) Promotes cell division
B) Helps in stem growth
C) Inhibits growth and causes wilting of leaves
D) Helps in phototropism
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: Which gland is responsible for secreting the hormone that regulates blood sugar levels?
A) Pituitary gland
B) Adrenal gland
C) Thyroid gland
D) Pancreas
Correct Answer: D

## MCQ 4
Question:  The gap between two neurons is called a:
A) Dendrite
B) Axon
C) Synapse
D) Receptor
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 5
Question:  Which hormone is responsible for the development of secondary sexual characteristics in males, such as the growth of a beard?
A) Insulin
B) Adrenaline
C) Thyroxine
D) Testosterone
Correct Answer: D
