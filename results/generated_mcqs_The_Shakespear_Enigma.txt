## MCQ 1
Question: What unusual characteristic did the fruits of the Shakespear tree possess?
A) They were shaped like apples.
B) They shimmered with a golden hue.
C) They tasted like grapes.
D) They grew only at night.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did the villagers believe the Shakespear tree could do?
A) Grant wishes
B) Heal illnesses
C) Give a glimpse into the future
D) Bring good luck
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: What was Emma's dream?
A) To become a painter
B) To become a writer
C) To become a dancer
D) To become a scientist
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 4
Question: What happened after Emma ate the Shakespear fruit?
A) She forgot her dreams.
B) She experienced a whirlwind of visions.
C) She fell asleep.
D) Nothing happened.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 5
Question: What became of Eldermere after Emma followed her dream?
A) It became a ghost town.
B) It became a hub of storytelling.
C) It became famous for its apples.
D) It became a popular tourist destination for its unique architecture.
Correct Answer: B
